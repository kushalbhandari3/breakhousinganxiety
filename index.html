As the Student Trustee at Springfield College, I am launching a project that tackles a deeply personal issue: the overwhelming stress, loneliness, and uncertainty faced by students who cannot go home during academic breaks due to financial, logistical, or safety concerns.

Objective

To provide students with a sense of security, belonging, and relief during some of their most vulnerable times by addressing break housing challenges.

Key Challenges

  • Financial Hardship: High travel costs and expensive housing fees.
  • Logistical Barriers: Storage difficulties and travel restrictions.
  • Safety Concerns: Unstable home environments and social isolation.
  • Community & Food Access: Limited affordable meal options and lack of social connection.

Our Approach

  • Housing Support: Waivers for campus stays for those in need.
  • Fundraising Efforts: Financial aid through the Student Emergency Fund.
