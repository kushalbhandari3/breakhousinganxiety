Dismantling Break Housing Anxiety

An Initiative by Kushal Bhandari

🌟 A Project Close to My Heart 🌟

As the Student Trustee at Springfield College, I’m launching a project to address a deeply personal issue: the stress, loneliness, and uncertainty faced by students who cannot go home during academic breaks. This initiative isn't just about housing—it's about security, community, and relief during vulnerable times.

Key Issues Addressed

  • Financial Barriers: High travel costs and expensive break housing fees.
  • Logistical Challenges: Storage issues and limited travel options.
  • Safety Concerns: Unstable home environments and isolation distress.
  • Food & Community Needs: Lack of affordable food and social support.

Our Plan for Change

  • Tier 1 - Giving Day (March 6, 2025): Raising funds for the Student Emergency Fund to support students facing financial hardships during breaks.
  • Tier 2 - Cultural Gala (April 11, 2025): A celebration of diversity, talent, and community to fundraise for housing assistance.
