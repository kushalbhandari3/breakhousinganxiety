Dismantling Break Housing Anxiety

By Kushal Bhandari

Objective

To alleviate housing stress for students who cannot return home during academic breaks due to financial, logistical, or safety concerns.

Key Issues Addressed

  • Financial Challenges: High airfare costs and expensive break housing fees.
  • Logistical Challenges: Storage issues and travel restrictions.
  • Safety Concerns: Unsafe home environments and isolation distress.
  • Food & Community Needs: Lack of affordable food options and social isolation.

Proposed Solutions

  • Housing Waivers: Free campus stays for students in need.
  • Donation System: Fundraising efforts for food and housing support.
